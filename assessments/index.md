## Exam Preparation Tips

1. **Understand the pipeline:** The exam tests your understanding of how each stage connects to the next. Can you explain what happens if the Kalman filter fails? How does that affect mapping and planning?

2. **Know the math:** Be comfortable with the Kalman filter equations, A* cost function, PID error terms, and Pure Pursuit geometry. The formula sheet helps, but you need to know *when* to apply each formula.

3. **Think in systems:** The best answers demonstrate understanding of the complete system, not just isolated components.

4. **Practice with code:** Review your lab implementations. The exam may ask you to trace through an algorithm by hand.
